State Wait Calculator

About


Statewait.com is a website created in order to calculate firearm delivery or pickup date after purchase from a Federal Firearm Licensed Dealer (FFL). Different states have different regulations regarding wait times after purchasing a firearm.

I created this website as an employee of an FFL in Washington State, which has complicated rules regarding pickup of a delayed firearm background check and purchase of a pistol without a concealed pistol license, in order to give me an accurate date to give the customer of when customers could pick up their firearm.

The 4473 expiry date calculator deals with the expiry date of the background form itself. The 4473 is good for only 30 days and must be filled out again if the firearm is not picked up within those 30 days. This website is merely a tool which suggests when you can pick up your firearm after purchase and should not be used in an official capacity. Please refer to your local ordinances to verify the pick up dates are correct.
